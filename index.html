⚠️ Plateau Troubleshooting

If weight doesn't move for 3+ weeks:
  • Check nutrition accuracy (use food scale, not eyeballing)
  • Increase training intensity (add 15 sec rest reduction)
  • Add 1 extra Stair Master session (15 mins on Wed/Thu)
  • Check sleep (7-9 hours? Stress level?)
  • Check hydration (2.5-3L daily?)
  • Only THEN reduce calories by 100 (not more!)
  • Check cycle (if female - normal to retain water)
💡 Most common cause: Food logging inaccuracy. Spend 1 week being ruthlessly honest with portions!
